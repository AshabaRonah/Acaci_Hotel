Meetings and Events


 

Choosing a right venue for your requirement is very important. Acacia Hotel is located right in the heart of Mbarara City within 10 minutes distance from the city center. With 5 closed venues and 1 open garden, we can provide you best location to host your event. Whether you want to invite 20 people for your important meeting or want to throw a musical treat to one hundred people, we can accommodate all. We are also the first choice for VIP wedding parties & ceremonies. Most of the large corporations based in Uganda loves to host their conferences with us. Rest assured, whatever your need be, Acacia Hotel would be able to elevate your experience and give your guests an unforgettable experience.
